What is Rider's Edge?

Rider's Edge was born from a deep passion for snowboarding and the need for a centralized platform where riders worldwide can share their most impressive clips. Whether you've mastered a `Double Cork 1080` in the park or found the perfect line in fresh powder, we want to see your work.

Our site isn't just a gallery; it's a vibrant community. We evaluate the originality, execution, and style of every submitted trick. By uploading your video, you enter the chance to be featured in our "Trick of the Week Gallery" and win exclusive prizes from our sponsors. The originality of your style is a key factor, so be creative!
